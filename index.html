About CrypTrees

Two Worlds Better Together

CrypTrees are a collection of 6,674 unique trees 🌳 made up with more than a Hundred Exquisite Traits. We are a team of four Computer Science Graduates trying to use our expertise for a good cause.
Nature Fungible Tokens begin here.

With a CrypTree, not only can you flaunt a Tree in the Digital World but you also hold a key role in planting trees in the Real World. With an aim to Revive Nature in this world, we donate 40% of our earnings to Nature Support Organizations,including Team Trees.
